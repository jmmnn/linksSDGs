#LinksSDGs

  • About

View the results of a machine-based literature review of 47 United Nations Publications.

From each publication a computer programme extracted and tagged all sentences relating to any Sustainable Development Goal.

Examples:
  • A sentence about poverty would be tagged as SDG 1 "No Poverty".
  • A sentence about health would be tagged as SDG 3 "Good Health & Well-being" etc.
